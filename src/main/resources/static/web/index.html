"Air Force 1", by "Sky Walkies"

Originally designed by Nike in 1982, the Sky Walkies "Airforce 1" marks the first time the shoe has been made outside of a Nike factory. An emblem of the street style community that sapwned it, Men's Artistic Director Renzo Balbo considered the Nike Air Force 1 a cultural symbol in its own right. Now two icons join forces to create the mos authentic high-end sports shoe. Fasion transcends to become an art object symbol of the subculture with a bit of magic and innovation.

Handcrafted

Travel to Fiesso d'Artico and enter the Maison's atelier to discover the unrivaled craftsmanship the most authentic high-end sports shoe ever.

"A PATTERN TAKES SHAPE"

A true craftsmanship is based on a skillful hand and precision. Patterns in luxury materials are cut, arranged and prepared for construction.

Designs Gallery

For Spring-Summer 2022/23, Renzo Balbo staged a collaboration between Sky Walkies and Nike, creating 47 bespoke "Air Force 1" editions. Each of them was expertly crafted, fusing the classic codes of the sneaker with the House's own insignials and materials, such as leather, cocodrile, synthetic leather and PVC among others.
